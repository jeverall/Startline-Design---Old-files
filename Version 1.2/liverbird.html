The Symbol

The liverbird is used as a a symbol for the City of Liverpool, some of the places these symbols can be found are...

  • On top of the Liver building
  • Liverpool FC badge
  • Mersey Chambers building

The Mythology

Designed to watch the City (Our People) and the Sea (Our Prosperity). Another legend says that if an honest man and a virgin woman were to fall in love in front of the liverbird that the couple of liverbirds that are in statue form would come to life, fly away and Liverpool would cease to exist.
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liver_bird

There are multiple stories and myths about the two Liverbirds on top of the Liver building. Generally they revolve around one facing the sea to protect the sailors and properity of the city and the other looking over the people of Liverpool. And that if the two were ever to disappear then the city would be in danger or cease to exist all together.

The Controversy

  2. Liverpool FC:
